FAQ

How are XLV tokens distributed?
Tokens are being manually released in batches following a fair linear distribution. Initial emissions to current GPU farms will be sent to the DEX to provide some liquidity for those not participating in the closed alpha.

Are 100% of tokens issued provided to a DEX LP or GPU farms?
Yes, there are no founder, advisor, marketing, development or VC funds, and no pre-sale or ICO of any kind. If others decide to join the network with their GPU running models with 97% uptime, they will be sent their portion of the daily emission.
